About The Firm

A Legacy Built on Trust

P.L. & Associates was established in 1995 in Chennai as a partnership firm of FCA professionals, now operating as a proprietary organisation. For over three decades, we have rendered quality professional services to a distinguished clientele.

Our firm has been subject to Peer Review Audit twice — in 2008 and in 2013 — reflecting our commitment to quality. We hold strategic ties with CA firms, Management Consultants, Cost Accountants, Company Secretaries, Lawyers, Engineers, and Architects across India.
