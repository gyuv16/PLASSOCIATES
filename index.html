About The Firm

A Legacy Built on Trust

P.L. & Associates was established in 1995 in Chennai as a partnership firm of FCA professionals. For over three decades, we have delivered quality professional services to a distinguished clientele spanning banks, corporations, trusts, and government institutions.

Our firm has been Peer Reviewed twice — in 2008 and in 2013 — a mark of our unwavering commitment to quality. We hold strong ties with CA firms, Management Consultants, Cost Accountants, Company Secretaries, Lawyers, Engineers, and Architects across India.

Our Services

Income Tax Matters

Service 02

Company Law Matters

Complete corporate compliance and statutory support under the Companies Act.

  • Company incorporation; LO/BO/PO registration for foreign entities
  • Board meeting, EGM & AGM minutes; annual returns filing
  • Formation of Societies and Trusts
  • Charge registration and statutory compliance inspections
Service 03

Statutory & Internal Audit

Independent, thorough audit services across banking and corporate sectors for over three decades.

  • Revenue and Stock Audit for nationalised & scheduled banks
  • Statutory and Internal Audits for companies, societies & trusts
  • Fixed asset verification, tagging & reconciliation
  • Third-party confirmations: intercompany, vendor & bank
Service 04

Foreign Exchange (FEMA)

Expert guidance on India's foreign exchange regulations, RBI approvals, and international investment matters.

  • FEMA compliance advisory and certification
  • FDI policy counsel and FIPB investment proposals
  • RBI approvals for share transfers & forex transactions
  • Technology transfer, EPC contracts, foreign currency loans
Service 05

Accounting Services

Full-spectrum bookkeeping, accounting management, and financial reporting services.

  • Invoice verification and salary advance monitoring
  • Books of account maintenance per statutory provisions
  • Monthly MIS Reports, Trial Balance & Annual Accounts
  • Bank, vendor & intercompany reconciliations
Service 06

Systems Audit & Advisory

Technology-focused audit with forensic investigation and financial project advisory capabilities.
